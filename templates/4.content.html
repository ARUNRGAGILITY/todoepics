Welcome to the Open-source Agile software for Agilists, Leaders, and Managers!

Agile is a mindset & practice that promotes an iterative, incremental, adaptive ways-of-working through which we get high-quality products/services/solutions and manage projects with short-feedback cycles. This agile approach helps us to achieve business agility and deliver business value to our consumers.

We present you a simple open-source agile software that can support adopting agile ways-of-working for your organization. You can download and install this software and customize as per your needs and also build required features or utilize as-is. We welcome your feedback, contribution and suggestions.

Basic features of this software are:
  • Manage Projects like Value Stream
  • Manage Products, Services and Solutions
  • Adapt LeanAgileSystem ways: Define, Refine, Prioritize, Visualize and Plan-Do-Check-Act
  • Adapt Scrum, Kanban, XP or customize your practice
  • Apply Agile @ Scale practices or adapt an existing Scaling or Custom Scaling Framework.
